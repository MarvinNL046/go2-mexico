## What to Expect

A sunset sailing cruise past [El Arco](/destinations/cabo-arch/) is one of the quintessential Cabo San Lucas experiences. As the sun drops toward the Pacific horizon, you sail from the marina past Land's End, where the iconic arch and surrounding rock formations glow in the golden light. Sea lions bask on the rocks, pelicans dive for fish, and -- during winter months -- humpback whales may breach in the distance.

Most cruises last 2-3 hours and include an open bar, appetizers or snacks, and music on board. Options range from large party catamarans to intimate sailboats.

## Cruise Options

### Party Catamarans
Large boats (30-100+ passengers) with open bars, music, and a social atmosphere. Fun for groups and those looking for an energetic experience.

### Sailing Yachts
Smaller boats (10-30 passengers) offering a more relaxed, intimate experience. Better for couples and those who prefer a quieter vibe.

### Private Charters
Rent an entire boat for your group. Available in various sizes. Best for special occasions.

### Luxury Yachts
High-end vessels with premium spirits, gourmet appetizers, and personalized service.

## What You Will See

- **El Arco** -- The arch is spectacular at sunset, with the light streaming through the opening
- **Sea lions** -- A colony on the rocks near Land's End
- **Lover's Beach and Divorce Beach** -- Visible from the water
- **Pelican Rock** -- A popular snorkeling spot (some daytime cruises stop here)
- **Whales** -- During December through March, humpback whales are frequently spotted

## Tips

- **Book in advance during peak season** -- December through March is busiest
- **Choose your vibe** -- Party boats and quiet sailboats are very different experiences. Read reviews.
- **Bring a light jacket** -- Temperatures cool down on the water after sunset
- **Motion sickness** -- The seas are usually calm in the bay, but take precautions if sensitive
- **Camera ready** -- The golden hour light on the arch is spectacular

## Cost

- **Party catamarans** -- 800-1,500 MXN per person including open bar and snacks
- **Sailing yachts** -- 1,200-2,500 MXN per person
- **Private charters** -- 5,000-25,000+ MXN depending on boat size and duration

## Booking

Tours depart from the Cabo San Lucas marina. Book through your hotel, online platforms, or walk to the marina where vendors sell tickets (though advance booking is recommended during peak season).
