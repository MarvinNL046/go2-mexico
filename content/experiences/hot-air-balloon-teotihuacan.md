## What to Expect

Floating above the ancient pyramids of [Teotihuacan](/destinations/teotihuacan/) at sunrise is one of the most spectacular experiences near [Mexico City](/cities/mexico-city/). As the sun rises over the Valley of Mexico, dozens of colorful balloons lift off simultaneously, offering aerial views of the Pyramid of the Sun, the Pyramid of the Moon, and the Avenue of the Dead from a perspective that reveals the ancient city's grand scale in a way the ground cannot.

Flights typically last 45-60 minutes and reach heights of 200-300 meters, depending on conditions. The experience is serene and quiet, with only the occasional blast of the burner interrupting the silence.

## The Experience

### Timeline
- **5:00-5:30 AM** -- Pickup from meeting point or Mexico City hotel (depending on operator)
- **6:00-6:30 AM** -- Arrive at the launch field near Teotihuacan
- **6:30-7:00 AM** -- Watch the balloon inflation and safety briefing
- **7:00-8:00 AM** -- Flight over the pyramids as the sun rises
- **8:00-8:30 AM** -- Landing, often followed by a toast with sparkling wine or coffee

Some operators include a subsequent visit to the archaeological site itself, which opens at 9:00 AM.

## Tips

- **Book well in advance** -- Balloon rides are one of the most popular activities near Mexico City, especially on weekends
- **Dress in layers** -- Early mornings can be cold at 2,200+ meters elevation. The air warms once the sun is up.
- **Wear closed-toe shoes** -- Required for boarding and landing
- **Charge your camera** -- The aerial views are spectacular. A phone camera works well.
- **Weather dependent** -- Flights are cancelled in high winds or rain. Operators typically reschedule or refund.
- **Not for everyone** -- If you have a strong fear of heights, consider whether this is right for you. The basket is stable and the flight is smooth, but you are high up with an open view.

## Cost

Balloon rides cost approximately 3,000-5,000 MXN per person depending on the operator, season, and whether transport from Mexico City and site entry are included. Weekend flights tend to cost more. Shared baskets hold 8-16 passengers; private flights are available at premium prices.

## Operators

Several companies operate from the Teotihuacan area. Check reviews, safety records, and what is included (transport, breakfast, site entry). Reputable operators are certified by civil aviation authorities.

## Getting There

Most operators offer hotel pickup from Mexico City, departing around 5:00-5:30 AM. If driving yourself, the launch sites are near the town of Teotihuacan, about 50 km northeast of Mexico City.

## Plan Your Visit

A balloon ride pairs perfectly with a visit to the [Teotihuacan](/destinations/teotihuacan/) ruins and is included in the [Mexico City 3-Day itinerary](/itineraries/mexico-city-3-days/) as an optional add-on.
