## Overview

This 5-day itinerary covers the highlights of [Baja California](/regions/baja-california/), from the dynamic food scene of Tijuana to the wine country of Valle de Guadalupe and the resort beaches of Los Cabos. Note: driving the full length of the Baja Peninsula takes 22+ hours, so this itinerary includes a domestic flight between Ensenada/Tijuana and Los Cabos.

## Day 1: Tijuana

- Morning: Cross the border from San Diego or fly into Tijuana (TIJ). Explore Avenida Revolucion and the Pasaje Rodriguez, a cultural corridor of galleries and cafes.
- Lunch: Baja Med cuisine at a Tijuana restaurant -- the fusion of Mexican, Mediterranean, and Asian flavors born here
- Afternoon: Craft beer tasting at Tijuana's growing brewery scene (Cerveceria Insurgente, Border Psycho, or others)
- Evening: Street tacos and more craft beer in the Zona Gastronomica

## Day 2: Ensenada and Valle de Guadalupe

- Morning: Drive south to Ensenada (1.5 hours). Stop at La Bufadora blowhole.
- Lunch: Fish tacos at the Ensenada fish market -- some say the battered fish taco was invented here
- Afternoon: Drive to Valle de Guadalupe (30 minutes inland). Wine tasting at 2-3 boutique wineries.
- Evening: Dinner at one of Valle de Guadalupe's acclaimed restaurants set among the vineyards. Stay overnight at a valley hotel or villa.

## Day 3: Valle de Guadalupe and Fly to Los Cabos

- Morning: More wine tasting or a visit to an olive oil producer in the valley
- Midday: Drive back to Tijuana airport (2 hours)
- Afternoon: Fly to Los Cabos (SJD) -- approximately 2.5 hours
- Evening: Arrive in Cabo San Lucas, check in, dinner on the marina

## Day 4: Los Cabos -- El Arco and Beaches

- Morning: Water taxi to [El Arco](/destinations/cabo-arch/) and Lover's Beach
- Late morning: Snorkeling at Playa Chileno or Playa Santa Maria
- Afternoon: Relax at [Medano Beach](/destinations/los-cabos-beaches/), the main swimmable beach in Cabo
- Evening: [Sunset sailing cruise](/experiences/sunset-sailing-cabo/) past El Arco -- an essential Cabo experience

## Day 5: San Jose del Cabo and Departure

- Morning: Drive to San Jose del Cabo (30 minutes). Walk the art district, visit the historic town center and church.
- Optional: If visiting January-March, join a [whale watching tour](/experiences/whale-watching-baja/) to see gray or humpback whales
- Lunch: Farm-to-table cuisine in San Jose del Cabo's restaurant district
- Afternoon: Transfer to airport for departure

## Practical Information

- **Budget estimate** -- $100-180 USD per day for mid-range travel
- **Transport** -- Rent a car for the Tijuana-Ensenada-Valle de Guadalupe portion. In Los Cabos, taxis and Uber are available.
- **Wine tasting** -- Tastings at Valle de Guadalupe wineries typically cost 200-600 MXN for a flight
- **Best time** -- October to May. January to March adds whale watching.
- **Border crossing** -- If starting from San Diego, use the CBX bridge for direct access to Tijuana airport area, or cross at San Ysidro.
