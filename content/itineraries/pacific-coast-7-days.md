## Overview

This 7-day itinerary explores the Banderas Bay region of Mexico's [Pacific Coast](/regions/pacific-coast/), centered on [Puerto Vallarta](/cities/puerto-vallarta/) and the charming beach towns of the Riviera Nayarit. Expect stunning sunsets, surf lessons, whale watching (in season), and a blend of resort comfort and bohemian beach culture.

## Day 1: Puerto Vallarta Arrival

- Afternoon: Arrive at PVR airport, transfer to hotel in the Zona Romantica
- Late afternoon: Walk the Malecon boardwalk, admire the public sculptures
- Evening: Dinner in the Zona Romantica -- try local seafood at a traditional restaurant

## Day 2: Puerto Vallarta Exploration

- Morning: Explore Old Town -- Church of Our Lady of Guadalupe, local markets
- Lunch: Fresh ceviche and fish tacos on the beach
- Afternoon: Los Muertos Beach for swimming and relaxation, or a boat trip along the coast
- Evening: Art walk (Wednesdays during high season) or live music at a Malecon bar

## Day 3: Marietas Islands

- Early morning: Boat tour to [Marietas Islands](/destinations/marietas-islands/) from Punta de Mita
- Morning: Snorkeling around the islands, visit Hidden Beach (if permits available)
- Afternoon: Return to shore, relax at a beach club in Punta de Mita
- Evening: Dinner in the coastal village of La Cruz de Huanacaxtle

## Day 4: Sayulita

- Morning: Transfer to [Sayulita](/destinations/sayulita/) (40 minutes from PV)
- Late morning: [Surf lesson](/experiences/surf-lesson-sayulita/) on the main beach
- Afternoon: Explore the town's colorful streets, street art, and boutiques
- Evening: Fish tacos and live music at the plaza, watch the sunset from the beach

## Day 5: Sayulita and San Pancho

- Morning: More surfing or visit Playa de los Muertos for quieter snorkeling
- Lunch: In San Pancho (10 minutes north) -- a quieter, artsy beach town
- Afternoon: Explore San Pancho's beach and community projects
- Evening: Return to Sayulita for dinner and the night market

## Day 6: Whale Watching or Sierra Madre

- **Option A (December-March):** Morning whale watching tour from Banderas Bay -- humpback whales
- **Option B:** Hike to a waterfall in the Sierra Madre foothills behind Puerto Vallarta
- Afternoon: Beach time or spa treatment
- Evening: [Sunset sailing](/experiences/sunset-sailing-cabo/) on Banderas Bay (several operators in PV)

## Day 7: Final Day and Departure

- Morning: Last beach morning, souvenir shopping at the Isla Cuale market
- Lunch: Final meal of pescado zarandeado or birria tacos
- Afternoon: Transfer to airport for departure

## Practical Information

- **Budget estimate** -- $80-140 USD per day for mid-range travel
- **Transport** -- Uber works in Puerto Vallarta. Buses run to Sayulita and surrounding towns. Renting a car is useful but not essential.
- **Best time** -- November to May for dry weather. December to March for whale watching.
- **Sayulita tip** -- Book accommodation in advance for weekends and holidays when the town fills with domestic visitors from Guadalajara
