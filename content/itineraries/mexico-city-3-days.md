## Overview

Three days in [Mexico City](/cities/mexico-city/) gives you enough time to hit the essential highlights of one of the world's greatest cities. This itinerary covers the must-see museums, neighborhoods, food experiences, and cultural attractions.

## Day 1: Centro Historico and Roma

- Early morning: Optional [hot air balloon ride over Teotihuacan](/experiences/hot-air-balloon-teotihuacan/) (requires 5:00 AM departure, returns by noon)
- Morning: Explore the Zocalo -- Metropolitan Cathedral, Templo Mayor Aztec ruins, National Palace (free entry to see Diego Rivera's murals)
- Lunch: [Street food tour](/experiences/street-food-tour-mexico-city/) through the centro -- tacos, tlacoyos, tamales
- Afternoon: Walk along Avenida Madero to the Palacio de Bellas Artes. Explore the Roma Norte neighborhood -- street art, cafes, Mercado Medellin
- Evening: Dinner in Roma or Condesa at one of the many acclaimed restaurants

## Day 2: Chapultepec, Museums, and Nightlife

- Morning: [Museo Nacional de Antropologia](/destinations/museo-nacional-de-antropologia/) -- plan for 3+ hours in this extraordinary museum
- Lunch: Polanco neighborhood for upscale Mexican cuisine
- Afternoon: [Chapultepec Castle](/destinations/chapultepec-castle/) for panoramic views and the National History Museum. Walk through Chapultepec Park.
- Evening: Choose your adventure --
  - [Lucha Libre](/experiences/lucha-libre/) at Arena Mexico (Tuesday, Friday, or Sunday)
  - [Plaza Garibaldi](/experiences/mariachi-plaza-garibaldi/) for live mariachi (any evening)
  - Mezcal bars in Roma for a quieter evening

## Day 3: Coyoacan, Xochimilco, and Teotihuacan

- **Option A: Coyoacan + Xochimilco**
  - Morning: [Frida Kahlo Museum](/destinations/frida-kahlo-museum/) (Casa Azul) -- book tickets well in advance. Explore Coyoacan's charming plazas and markets.
  - Lunch: Coyoacan market
  - Afternoon: [Xochimilco boat ride](/experiences/xochimilco-boat-ride/) through the floating gardens on a trajinera

- **Option B: Teotihuacan + Coyoacan**
  - Early morning: [Teotihuacan](/destinations/teotihuacan/) -- climb the Pyramids of the Sun and Moon (2-3 hours at the site)
  - Lunch: On-site or en route back to the city
  - Afternoon: Frida Kahlo Museum and Coyoacan exploration
  - Evening: Farewell dinner in Condesa

## Practical Information

- **Budget estimate** -- $60-120 USD per day for mid-range travel
- **Transport** -- Metro (5 MXN per ride), Metrobus, and Uber/DiDi cover the city efficiently. Walk in Roma, Condesa, Coyoacan, and the centro.
- **Book ahead** -- Frida Kahlo Museum and Teotihuacan balloon rides require advance booking
- **Best time** -- March to May for dry, warm weather. Late October for Day of the Dead.
- **Stay in** -- Roma Norte or Condesa for the best combination of location, dining, and walkability
